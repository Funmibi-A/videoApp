How to Request Account Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. Follow these simple steps to request deletion of your [Your App Name] account and associated data:

  1. Send an email to our data privacy team using the button below
  2. Include your registered email address or username associated with your [Your App Name] account
  3. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Username/Email]"
